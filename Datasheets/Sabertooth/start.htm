Sabertooth DIP switch wizard



This wizard will help you to quickly configure your Sabertooth 2X25, 2X10 or 2X5 to meet your needs. We will ask you a few questions about your project, and based on your answers we will show you the recommended DIP switch settings that will make things work for you. Ok, ready for the first question?

What kind of batteries are you using?
  • Lithium Ion / Lithium Polymer
  • Other such as Lead-acid, NiCd, NiMH
This question affects whether or not the lithium cutoff mode will be selected (switch 3).
